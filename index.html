Hook — The Student Spiral

Every student knows the pattern — less sleep, more caffeine, higher stress. This prototype turns those daily choices into visuals you can explore.

Novel Vis: Coping Strategy Garden

Stem height = stress reduction effectiveness (taller = lower average stress). Leaf color = frequency. Flower size = avg exercise hours.

Planned Interactions

  • Hover for tooltips (implemented)

Emotion Constellation Triangle

Each dot is a student positioned by their normalized Stress (PSS-10), Anxiety (GAD-7), and Depression (PHQ-9) proportions. Color encodes combined severity.
